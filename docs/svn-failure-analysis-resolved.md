## 1. Passive Value Farming
**Original concern:** Bots scrolling to farm value.
**YOUR ANSWER:** You do NOT get value by scrolling. You get value by reading and then adding something coherent based on what you've read. A computer/AI can verify whether the response is actual logic.

## 2. Carnivorous Loop Dominance
**Original concern:** Everyone becomes a predator, no one creates.
**YOUR ANSWER:** Predators are natural and necessary. They eliminate bullshit — there's value in that. Consumption yields less energy than creation, but it's still valuable. Plants photosynthesize — they're originals. Growth is the goal, not predation. Both roles are necessary.
**RESOLVED:** The ecosystem needs both. Consumption costs energy and yields less than creation, so pure predation is self-limiting. The system naturally balances because creators (photosynthesizers) are the primary energy generators. Predators serve a cleaning function.

## 3. Unequal Starting Energy

## THE AI's FIRST RESPONSE

When you plant something, the first response is always from the AI. It evaluates immediately and scores:

**What the AI scores for:**

| Category | What It Means |
|---|---|
| Logic | Is this structurally sound? Does the reasoning hold? |
| Rhetoric | Is this using persuasion techniques instead of logic? (Negative score) |
| Nostalgia | Is this appealing to "the good old days" without structural basis? (Negative score) |
| Emotional appeal | Is this using pathos instead of logos? (Flag, not automatic negative) |
| Novelty | Is this a genuinely new insight or a repackaged common take? |
| Verifiability | Can this be checked? Are the claims falsifiable? |
| Scope | Is this appropriately scoped or making claims beyond its evidence? |

The AI doesn't just say "good" or "bad." It tells you WHAT it is. "This holds a lot of weight" or "This scores high on rhetoric — here's where the persuasion mechanics are."

You don't score yourself. The AI scores you. Automatically. Before any human even sees it.

## CORE PHILOSOPHY

### The Problem This Solves

The world is run by people using rhetoric without logic. Leaders who speak on behalf of communities without cognitive ability to verify what they're saying. Laws made by people who benefit from making them. A loop of corruption.

Most people see this as too big to solve. It IS huge. The only way to solve it is to break it completely down. Remove their words so they have no value. The Three Monkeys Lens and the Alpha-Omega rhetoric lens identify when someone is so unaware of reality that by speaking, they reveal they can't tie their own shoes.

### The Root Problem

Bad memetics — passed down from parents, grandparents, generations. Unexamined inherited beliefs treated as truth. This virus of unverified thinking is so valueless to society that it's actually harmful. When someone spreads it, they're vomiting out material that's only good for recycling (compost).

The cure: not speaking. Crawling into a hole and listening. The ego is the reason people celebrate their own bullshit.

### What This System Values

Cognitive ability — the capacity to see where you fit in the world. Can you see that one action ripples into another? Have you looked at the other side? Have you ever been proved wrong? If you've NEVER been proved wrong, that's the first sign something is wrong.

## THE BIOLOGY MODEL (Corrected)

This system follows biological PATTERNS, not a biological blueprint.

### Energy Sources

**Sunlight = Identity Verification**
- Validates you're probably a human
- All verified humans start with the same base energy
- More linked accounts = bigger window = more capacity to absorb (not more sun)
- A bigger leaf absorbs more sun if it's in the sun-absorbing area, but it can also be less efficient
- Plants that grow really fast are often weak — they haven't built up strength (like an ant's cuticle before cold weather)
- "There is no root, and it withers away when the rains stop coming"

**Compost = Failed/Consumed Posts**
- First-come-first-served — like caterpillars on a defenseless plant
- If a post about religion gets logically dismantled, whoever gets there first consumes it
- Decomposition is passive (leaves drop yearly), but consumption is active

**Joining = Symbiotic Contribution**
- NOT "feeding" — contributors JOIN you
- Like the sandalwood tree and its host — they work together
- 37 trillion cells + 37 trillion symbiotic cells = one working body
- That's not doing all the work yourself — that's working systematically, as a whole

**Energy Degradation**
- Energy degrades over time — use it or lose it
- Like deciduous plants shedding leaves yearly
- New growth each cycle
- Some plants (evergreens) never drop their leaves — high performers retain energy

### The Predator Model

Predators are part of life. Every animal is a predator. There is nothing wrong with a carnivorous plant.

- Predators eliminate bullshit — that's their job, and there's value in it
- "I eliminate bullshit, it's my job" — that IS value
- A person who can identify and dismantle bullshit has just as much growth potential as an original creator
- "That's not true, I don't know the answer, but that's sure as fuck not the one" — THAT is value
- Consumption takes energy (it costs something to eat)
- Consumption yields less energy than original creation — but it's still necessary
- You do NOT have to plant something yourself before you can consume — you just need energy

### What Doesn't Work

- Rhetoric cannot consume rhetoric — only logic can
- Skilled rhetoric "doesn't do shit" against logic
- Plants photosynthesize — they're originals, they don't need predation to grow
- Growth is the goal, not predation

## IDENTITY VERIFICATION — "SUNLIGHT GATHERING"

Identity doesn't make you powerful. It validates you're probably human. Each verification source is a window that lets sunlight in.

### The Question: What Are All The Verification Pieces?

Sources that validate humanness and add window size:

**Tier 1 — Core Identity (Base Energy)**
- Email (verified)
- Phone number (verified)

**Tier 2 — Social Proof (Window Expansion)**
- Facebook (linked) — friends, family connections, history
- Reddit (linked) — post history, comment history, account age
- Instagram (linked) — visual presence, social connections

**Tier 3 — Deep History (Extended Window)**
- Gaming profiles — Google Play, League of Legends (10-year account = deep history), Steam, Xbox, PSN
- YouTube channel — established presence, content history
- Professional profiles — LinkedIn, portfolio sites

**Tier 4 — Personal Verification**
- Profile photos
- Profile information (bio, location, etc.)
- Family member connections (on linked platforms)

### What AI Does With This

AI can look deep into all of it and say:
- "Here's what we know about this person"
- "Here's what we think we know based on what others said about them"
- "They stood behind X in the past — how do they stand now?"
- "They've been wrong here and never recanted — that's ego, that's a negative checkmark"

Claims are either verifiable or not. If you still believe in flat earth, that tells the system something about your cognitive baseline.

### The Ego Problem

If anywhere in your history you've been wrong and haven't recanted it — that adds ego. Standing behind something demonstrably false is a big negative checkmark. The system tracks this.
